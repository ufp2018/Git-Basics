Courtney R. Gibbons earned a B.A. in mathematics from the 
Colorado College in 2006 and an M.S. and Ph.D. in commutative algebra 
from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2009 and 2013, respectively. She
completed her dissertation under the supervision of Roger Wiegand and Luchezar
L. Avramov. In July, 2013, she joined the mathematics faculty at Hamilton College 
and she remains an active scholar in commutative homological algebra.
